Welcome to Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty’s

Mission Niramaya Portal

The aim of this platform is to bring most of its student and institute management processes online, and to turn SMF into a competency-focussed regulator of human resources in health.

The Hon’ble Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh, Shri Yogi Adityanath, has envisioned the ambitious goal of turning UP into a hub of quality nursing and paramedical education in the state.

In order to realize this vision, Mission Niramaya was formulated by UP State Medical Faculty, under the aegis of the Department of Medical Education, and was subsequently launched by the CM on 8th October 2022. A bouquet of reforms are envisioned under this Mission, which are going to be enabled through a population scale technological platform.
